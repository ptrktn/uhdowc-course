NB: example-frontend build fails with ubuntu:18.04, an effective
    workaround is to use ubuntu:latest image.

Before:

docker image ls | grep -w -E '(example-backend|example-frontend)' | awk '{ print $1, $(NF) }'
example-backend 651MB
example-frontend 878MB

After:

docker image ls | grep -w -E '(example-backend|example-frontend)' | awk '{ print $1, $(NF) }'
example-backend 612MB
example-frontend 691MB
